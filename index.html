About Me

Hi!

I'm a Portuguese game developer with professional game industry experience. I'm currently working at Saber Interactive Porto.

I have experience in multiple areas of game development and have been working in both pc and console games. I started as a 3d artist well before joining the games development industry. Since then, I've wandered into more technical tasks, working on game design and programming, but especially in technical art. Those are roles that I've performed, in some form or another, in every project I was part of. My real passion became the combination of art and programming. As a technical artist is where I feel I can do my best work..

The takeaway is that I have a passion for game development and working deep within game engines like Unreal Engine 4, which I've worked with for the last eight years.

Digital Aquarium


This was a project developed for Sesimbra's Maritime Museum. It tries to reproduce Sesimbra's maritime wildlife in its natural habitat. It allows its visitors to know more about all the different species that inhabit those waters. This is an interactable rendition of Sesimbra's aquatic life, where each species has its own set of rules to navigate the underwater environment and interact with each other.

Digital Aquarium video

In this project, I was responsible for the environment 3d modeling and composition, as well as some of the aquatic species 3d modeling and rigging. I also did the integration in Unreal Engine 4, all the animal artificial intelligence and rules, as well as all VFX and materials.

Dakar Desert Rally


Dakar Desert Rally is the biggest and most epic off-road rally racing adventure ever developed, coming in 2022 to PlayStation®5, Xbox Series X|S, PlayStation®4, Xbox One, and PC. This next-generation racer features true off-road rally action from the official 2020-2021 Dakar raids with authentic vehicles, realistic physics, dynamic seasons, and weather.

Dakar Desert Rally Website

Dakar Desert Rally Announce Trailer

For this project, I worked as a technical artist helping bring to life the open world of the Dakar Desert Rally. I was responsible for the vehicles particle effects as well as the weather particles, materials, and rendering effects. The big open world of this game requires big amounts of work to fully get the amount of detail it needs. With that in mind, I developed multiple in-engine tools to help the environment team populate and give life to that world. I also implemented and fixed several features in Unreal Engine's codebase, mostly on the rendering side, to perfectly bring forward the eye candy and features needed for this game.
